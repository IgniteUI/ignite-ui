Bookmark support is one of the key features of the jQuery Video Player control. The following sample demonstrates how to add bookmarks to the widget during initialization. If for some reason you don't have the Modernizr library available on your page, then the igVideoPlayer control is rendered, which in some cases may have fewer touch capabilities than the default device video player. That's why this sample uses the Modernizr library and in mobile contexts the igVideoPlayer falls back to the default device video player.
